Have your Cannacoins always with you, in your pocket! You pay by quickly scanning a QR-code. As a merchant, you receive payments reliably and instantly. Cannacoin Wallet is the first mobile Cannacoin app, and arguably also the most secure!

FEATURES:

• No registration, web service or cloud needed! This wallet is de-centralized and peer to peer.
• Display of Cannacoin amount in CCN and mCCN.
• Conversion to and from national currencies.
• Sending and receiving of Cannacoin via NFC, QR-codes or Cannacoin URLs.
• Address book for regularly used Cannacoin addresses.
• When you're offline, you can still pay via Bluetooth.
• System notification for received coins.
• App widget for Cannacoin balance.

You can find more about Cannacoin at
http://cannacoin.cc

If you want to contribute to Cannacoin Wallet, the project is located at
https://github.com/cannacoin-project/cannacoin-wallet

This is a fork of the popular Bitcoin Wallet by Andreas Schildbach and was converted to Cannacoin by me.
Find the original at
https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=de.schildbach.wallet
